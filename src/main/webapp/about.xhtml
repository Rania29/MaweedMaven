ABOUT

We came with Maweed website idea because we want to give answers to the biggest question (WHERE ?).

Every one is asking, Where to have the best medical service? Where is the nearest hospital? Where is the best offers and much more.

We aim to provide the biggest and most up-to-date hospital and health care directory in Qatar. That provide more details about clinics, doctors they have, its services, offers and many details and information’s you need.

Another goal is to give people outside Qatar to have the ability to check the variety of health care centers and hospitals we have and to be able to request an appointment after checking all options suits them before they come to Qatar.
